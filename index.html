Projects Second Year

The First Project of my Second Year (Gaming Campus Second Year)

MathsStaticLibrary

This project is a complete mathematical library written in C++, designed to provide a solid foundation for vector,
matrix, and quaternion calculations, applied to both 2D and 3D transformations.

It was developed with the goal of gaining a deeper understanding of modern C++ (C++17) and experimenting with 3D concepts,
while also including an SFML 3.0.2 demonstration project (Pong‑style) that uses the library to handle geometric and physics computations.
Made in two weeks.

The Second Project of my Second Year (Gaming Campus Second Year)

THE PINK PARADOX

The Pink Paradox is a 2D Runner project made in C++ using SFML 3.0.2.
Lore: You play as an astronaut named John Cenna, who is trying to escape the Hello Kitty dimension he grew up in!
He must run through different dimensions and buy his freedom to break free from the loop of space and time.
We have learn here the importance of 3C in video games (Character, Controls, Camera).
I have worked on the player, physics and sound design.
